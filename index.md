# Privacy Policy — Lokey

**Last updated: May 2026**

---

## Overview

Lokey is an offline personal encryption utility. This privacy policy explains how the application handles your data. The short version: Lokey collects nothing, transmits nothing, and stores everything locally on your device under your control.

---

## Data Collection

Lokey does not collect any data. There is no analytics, no crash reporting, no usage tracking, and no telemetry of any kind. The application has no mechanism to send data anywhere because it makes no network connections.

---

## Data Transmission

Lokey never transmits any data over the internet or any network. The application operates entirely offline. No information about you, your contacts, your keys, or your messages ever leaves your device through Lokey.

---

## Data Stored on Your Device

Lokey stores the following data locally on your device only:

**Contact metadata** — the names you assign to contacts and the date they were added. This is stored in your device's local application storage and is never transmitted anywhere.

**Encryption keys** — cryptographic keys are stored exclusively in your device's hardware-backed secure storage (Android Keystore). These keys are protected by your device's biometric authentication or PIN. They are not accessible to other applications and cannot be extracted without your authentication credentials.

**Message history** — if you choose to save encrypted message blobs to history, those blobs are stored in local application storage. They are stored in their encrypted form only — Lokey never stores decrypted message content.

---

## Data You Share Outside Lokey

When you copy an encrypted message blob or share an encrypted file, you are sharing data through a third-party application (such as a messaging app or email client). That data is encrypted by Lokey before sharing. Lokey has no control over and no knowledge of what you do with encrypted output after it leaves the application. The privacy practices of those third-party applications are governed by their own privacy policies.

---

## Permissions

Lokey requests the following device permissions:

**Biometric / device authentication** — required to unlock the application and protect access to stored keys.

**Camera** — used only for scanning QR codes when adding a new contact. Camera access is not used for any other purpose. No images are captured or stored.

**Photo library / media access** — used only when you explicitly choose to select an image or video for encryption, or when saving a decrypted file to your gallery. Lokey does not access your media library at any other time.

---

## Children

Lokey is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the updated version will be published at the same URL with a new effective date. Because Lokey collects no data, changes to this policy will be limited to clarifications about application functionality.

## Summary

Lokey is a local tool. It has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no internet connectivity. Your keys stay on your device. Your messages are encrypted before they ever leave Lokey. The application cannot access your data and has no interest in doing so.
